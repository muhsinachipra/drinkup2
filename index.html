    About Drink Up

    Drink Up was a successful PHA initiative that encourage everyone to drink more water.The Drink Up campaign led to water being the most consumed purchased beverage in the country.

    PHA collaborated with PHA Honorary Chairwoman Michelle Obama and PHA partners to make the healthy choice the easy choice for busy parents and families.

    Drink Up had a wide variety of supporters and stakeholders across the private and public sectors who are dedicated to encouraging people to drink more water more often. View the full list of partners that helped make this amazing success possible.
